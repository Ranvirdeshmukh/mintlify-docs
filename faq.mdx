# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is SignPact?
SignPact is a comprehensive document signing and management platform that streamlines the entire document lifecycle from creation to storage.

### Is SignPact suitable for my business size?
Yes! SignPact is designed to serve businesses of all sizes, from solopreneurs to enterprise organizations. We offer different plans to accommodate various needs and usage volumes.

### How secure is SignPact?
SignPact employs industry-leading security measures including end-to-end encryption, secure data centers, regular security audits, and compliance with global security standards.

## Signatures & Legal Questions

### Are electronic signatures legally binding?
Yes. SignPact's electronic signatures comply with major e-signature laws worldwide, including ESIGN Act (US), eIDAS (EU), and similar regulations in other countries.

### What makes a signature valid in SignPact?
A valid signature in SignPact includes authentication of the signer's identity, clear intent to sign, and a record of the signing process which creates an audit trail.

### Can I use SignPact for international contracts?
Yes, SignPact supports international signatures and complies with e-signature laws in multiple jurisdictions.

## Technical Questions

### What file formats does SignPact support?
SignPact supports multiple file formats including PDF, Word documents (.docx), Excel spreadsheets, and text files.

### Can I integrate SignPact with my existing tools?
Yes, SignPact offers integrations with popular business tools and platforms. We also provide an API for custom integrations.

### What happens if someone doesn't have internet access?
Recipients can sign documents on any device with internet access. For those without immediate access, the signing request remains available until completed.

## Account & Billing Questions

### How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
You can change your plan at any time from your account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades apply at the end of your current billing cycle.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major credit cards, ACH transfers, and wire transfers for enterprise customers.

### Is there a free trial?
Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial with access to most features so you can test SignPact before committing.
